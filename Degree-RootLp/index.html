Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Roots Degree College and its programs — answered clearly for you.

Roots Degree College offers undergraduate programs including BBA, B.Com, and BA, with multiple specialisations.

All undergraduate programs are 3 years in duration, structured across semesters.

Programs include projects, workshops, and industry interactions to help students gain practical exposure.

Students receive academic guidance and career support to help them plan their professional paths.

The campus provides classrooms, learning resources, and student activity spaces that support both academics and extracurricular engagement.

Graduates can pursue careers in business, finance, media, marketing, analytics, or higher studies.
